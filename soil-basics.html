Soil Basics

Good landscaping starts with soil. Before planting, I check drainage, texture, and sunlight in the area.

  • Drainage test: water a small hole and see if it drains within a few hours.
  • Texture: sandy drains fast, clay holds water, loam is the goal.
  • Amendments: compost improves most soils and supports healthy roots.
