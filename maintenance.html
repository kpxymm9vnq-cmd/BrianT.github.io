Maintenance

I keep maintenance simple: consistent watering early, mulch, and basic pruning when needed.

  • Watering: new plants need steady water for the first few weeks.
  • Mulch: helps control weeds and keeps soil moisture stable.
  • Pruning: remove dead growth and shape lightly instead of over-cutting.
